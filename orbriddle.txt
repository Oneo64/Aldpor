(old and outdated version of norvunic, it was mostly a relex)

A jade orb in a sylvan forest,
sits on a great marble pedestal.
When the full moon shines on it,
Move it to the highest tower.

Od jadre urul ei od seilvan traevos,
hat jan od vost mobre lievaer.
Ag tamr od tif arandas tif jan ga,
ateyr ga od navir'rui vertat.

(new version of norvunic, no longer a relex)

Ûn jadre orul ern ûn silven trævos,
hat ug ûn bag mobre leivær.
Lanyr pa ik gevfde arandas svusor hom ug sat,
atr pa orul vral pa galaje vertat.
