Year 952
Antörias is born.

Year 954
Vielthöri is born.

Year 963
Vielthöri is discovered to be attuned to Lûnanyr after being able to detect a thief who had stolen a silver purse last night.

Year 970
Vielthöri starts practicing with spatha and kite shield.

Year 971
Vielthöri decides to paint her shield blue. She also got a surcoat, and dyed it purple using a portion of her family's wealth.

Year 972
Vielthöri senses a group of undead approaching from an unknown direction. She alerted the guards, but they did not believe her. About ten minutes later, the group of undead, made entirely of vamerks enter Pendlas Mus. The guards gathered to kill the vamerks, and Vielthöri assisted them, killing every undead. She felt extremely, and unusually uncomfortable the presence of these vamerks, and could sense them from a hundred meters away. After this, she began to exessively dislike undeath.
