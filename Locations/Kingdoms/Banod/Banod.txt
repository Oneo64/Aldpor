Government: Elected Diarchy
Race Majority: Dwarf (red metals and jewelry variants)
Culture type: Slavic

Main languages: Banodic

Rulers (guys):
	Olk (Rules economy, imports, exports, trading)
	Norad (Rules military, the law, criminal punishment)
	Yarandin (Major King, rules everything)

The castle of Banod is a slavic castle in a similar style of Garibaldi Castle. It is built on a peak on a steep mountain. The main entrance is a road up the mountain with some carved stairs on the steep parts.
