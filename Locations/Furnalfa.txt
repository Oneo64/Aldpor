Furnalfa is a plane of fey, or more commonly known as Realm of Pure Nature. It is a neverending bright forest of crooked oak, covered in moss. The ground is covered with plants, ferns, short grass, and sometimes, rocks. Most of these oaks do not have leaves, and if they do, it is very little, and the leaves are orange.

Inhabitants: Centaur, Dryad, Satyr, Sprite.
