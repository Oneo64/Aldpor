The Academy of Aldorim is an evocation school in Dagscald, located about 5 kilometers southeast of Thuryngard. It is a small school, about 900 cubic meters in area. It is made of two houses and a larger house, surrounded by low a wooden wall about 3 meters in height. The houses inside have thatched roofs, and the walles are coated in wattle and daub. The two houses are the sleeping areas for students, and the larger house is the main academy.

The walls of the academy are protected by wooden slabs carved with the symbol Repel Dead (Greater Runic Staves), each with a spacing of 10 meters.
