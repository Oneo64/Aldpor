He has a baldric carrying a scabbard with a 5-lobed viking sword on his belt, and his belt carries two leather pouches. Under the baldric he carries a small seax, and a waterskin. He also carries a 7 cm wooden orb in diameter, hung on the belt in a pouch made for it, and is carried on his back side to the left. He wears a necklace of Talmundur's symbol (silver) with a bunch of labradorite beads (10 to be precise). He is 178 (5' 10") centimeters tall.

He has a scar on his left shoulder, resulting in a scar and weakening that arm.
