Vielthöri Aldriàndel
Female
Age: 19 (born 954, march 5)
Alignment: Lawful Good
Ethnicity: Aodesvic
Race: Human aligned to Lûnanyr

Vielthöri is a celestial human who magically leans towards Lûnanyr, and was born in Pendlas Mus. She has vibrant green eyes, fair skin and long, light blond hair. She wears a purple surcoat bearing Lûnanyr's symbol, a grey tunic, brown pants, a belt, and a pair of boots. She also has a scabbard on her belt. She is 160 centimeters tall.

She carries a spatha in a blue scabbard, and a blue norman kite shield slung across her back.

Her goal is to "protect" the world and the cycle of life from undeath. She despises all types of necromancy unless it is only used to heal.
