Þrein Yggyrðandi
Male
Age: 45 (born 927, September 10)
Alignment: Lawful Good
Ethnicity: Dagsvaldian
Race:      Human

Þrein is a human from Lismerlav. He has grey eyes, pale skin, and short light brown hair. His face has a minimal amount of freckles. Þrein wears a muted aqua (blueish green) tunic, baggy brown pants, a belt, and a pair of boots. He wears an iron amulet of Hirulfyr's symbol.

Þrein is the father of Ennu. Þrein also works as a ferryman.
