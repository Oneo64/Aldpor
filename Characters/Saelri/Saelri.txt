Saelri is a druid from Vesesvala. She has vibrant green eyes, pale skin, and golden hair. Like all cold elves, her earlobes are long. She has a white cloak, wears a long dark aqua tunic, a belt, and a pair of shoes. She has a sheathed dagger on her belt, and a pouch. She also has a coin purse, carrying a few gold and about 200 silver.

Saelri is immune to all poison and disease, due to druidic practices. She also knows about Furnalfa.

She carries around a pine wood staff, tipped with a small amber orb.
