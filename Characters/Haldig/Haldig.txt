Haldig Dalsdavu I
Male
Age: 185 (born 788, March 10)
Alignment: Chaotic Good
Ethnicity: Daggsvaldian
Race:      Draugr (Human)

Haldig I was a human, born in 788 AD in Arggonlav. At 30 years old, he traveled down to Tarib with a handful of explorers. Halfway there, he encountered a daeva tribe, and was slain by them. He did not recieve a proper burial, and many years later, he became a draugr. Haldig I has old, black-green skin, saturated yellow eyes, and his hair is shoulder-length and black. He is 178 centimeters tall.

He wears a worn deer hide cloak, a nasal helmet, a worn faded red tunic, faded brown pants, a belt and a pair of shoes. He carries a dane axe, and uses it to walk as a walking stick.

Like all draugr, his voice is raspy and deep.
