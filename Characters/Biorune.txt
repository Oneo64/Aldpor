Biörûne Vaond

Female
Age: 11 (born 962, June 29)
Alignment: Neutral Good
Ethnicity: Esrel
Race:      Cold Elf



[[DESCRIPTION]]
Biörûne is a cold elf from Vesesvala. She has green pupiless eyes, pale skin, and blonde hair. Like all cold elves, her earlobes are long. She wears a tan triangular fur cap, long dark aqua tunic, a belt, and a pair of shoes.
