Meyrúna

Female
Age: 20 (born 953)
Alignment: Neutral Good
Ethnicity: Dagsvaldian
Race:      Human



[[DESCRIPTION]]
Meyrúna is a human from Argánlaf. She has wavy light brown hair which is shoulder-length, her skin is pale with a red hue, and has a few freckles on her face. Her eyes are large, with a pale green color. She is 162 (5'4) centimeters tall.

She wears a dark yellow woolen cloak with a slightly frayed edge, a muted blue dress, a ring belt made of braided leather, and leather shoes. On her belt there is a small seax in it's sheath. She wears a necklace with an iron Talmundur amulet.

Meyrúna is friends with Ljorn and Yngvild. She can usually be found in the nearby areas of the forest gathering herbs alone or with Ljorn, at her house making wood carvings, or by the river.



[[PERSONALITY]]
Meyrúna is described as calm and pleasant, however she can be introverted as she prefers to work on her carvings, or be at quiet areas such as near the river where there isn't much noise. She can also be generous, as she would sometimes gift wood carvings to people. She can also be unassertive, shown when she would avoid conflict by not associating herself with it. She has a slight crush on Ljorn.

She likes to eat a stew of chicken and local spices.



[[ABILITIES]]
Meyrúna can only speak Dagsvaldian, and picked up a minor English understanding due to traders and merchants.

Meyrúna can play a lyre and sometimes make up songs to play them, which are almost always in Dagsvaldian. Most of these songs are about nature or other people, however she would only share these songs with people close to her such as family and friends.

Meyrúna practices herbology and can identify many common herbs in the Northern region. She took interest in carving wood, and can turn them into things like spoons and small figurines to gift to other people.
