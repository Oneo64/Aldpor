Àlantre Rûmelanassa
Female
Age: 40, looks 20 (born 933, January 19)
Alignment: Lawful Good
Ethnicity: Rànûdan

Àlantre is a Moon Elf from Ailenfliiwem. She now traveled and now lives in Vuntarla, as a herb merchant. She has pale blue skin, dark purple hair, blue pupiless eyes. Her ears are long and pointed. She wears a brown calf-length tunic with tan accents, a ring belt, and leather shoes. She has a sheathed seax on her belt that's 12 inches long, and carries a spear slung across her back. She is 178 centimeters tall.

Her horse is an appaloosa named Fala, whose fur is spotted which makes it look like an intense case of freckles.
