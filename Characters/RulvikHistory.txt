Lorvon took interest in Swordfighting. He got his sword from the storage room, which was his grandfather's service weapon. Alf took interest of a spear.

Year 1032
Lorvon goes on first adventure to recover a book documenting the history of a lost civilization. Alf goes with him. There were also a couple other people. While looking for this book, a group of 12 draugr attacks the party. Alf gets cut, on the left leg, and Lorvon almost dies.
During the adventure, Lorvon discovers a tome that teaches the basics of magic, but doesn't read it. Instead, he throws it in the storage room of his home.

Year 1033
A raid group led by Jûmlaanar Elves attacks Argonlav. The family's home nearly gets destroyed. Lorvon wanted to protect his home and make sure it would never get destroyed, so he got the old tome, and taught himself. A few months later, he joined a school of evocation in the City of Norvun. While at the academy, he invented a silly game where you chuck wooden beads soaked in spark potion at each other. Being hit with a bead means you're out.

Year 1035
Lorvon and Alf both travel to Thalmundol (town).
Lorvon joins Norvun's army, while Alf went to some kind of training school. (might change the part with Alf later)

Lorvon learns some more advanced spells, but not very powerful. After some time, the Kingdom of Norvun went to war with an incoming invasion of Jûmlaanar elves from the south. He served as artillery from a distance.

After the battle ended, Lorvon and Alf both returned home.

Year 1036
Lorvon decides to go on another adventure, knowing he can fight as a wizard.

Year 1037
Lorvon returns from the adventure. He decides to build a better house for his family, using the best materials available. Along with himself, he paid an additional 10 workers to build the house. He also bought a 300gp crystal opal orb, as a new arcane focus.
