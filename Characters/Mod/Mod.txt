Mod Gorzadun
Male
Age: 100 (born 873, april 1)
Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Ethnicity: Banodic
Race:      Dwarf

Mod is a dwarf from Banod. He has blood red hair, and a beard braided into one, clamped by a silver clasp. His eyes are red, and his skin is pale and slightly dark. He wears a nasal helmet, black shawl, bronze hauberk, black shirt, baggy blue pants, and shoes. He also wears a belt, which has a coin purse attached. He is 130 centimeters tall.

Mod carries an engraved war hammer, and a bronze shield carved in Banodic that says "Balkrozstuul-mod", meaning "Mod's Shield", "balkrozs" meaning "shield" and "tuul" meaning "of".

His coin purse contains about 15 silver.
