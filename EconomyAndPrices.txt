Currencies:
	gp = 10 sp, 100 cp
	sp = 0.1 gp, 10 cp
	cp = 0.01 gp, 0.1 sp

Wealth: For personal wealth, divide by 2 and round down, or convert to cp, divide by three and round down. Middle class isn't exactly poor, but average.

Gold: Gold is equal to 10 silver, as you see above. However, gold coins are rare, so only the wealthy have some.

Average family wealth per social class (Personal wealth average divide by 4):
	Low class (peasants, serfs, slaves): family: 20 sp  + 20 cp              || personal: 5 sp   + 5 cp
	Middle class (karls):                family: 100 sp + 100 cp             || personal: 25 sp  + 25 cp
	--WEALTHY/RICH BORDER---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	High class (wealthy people, mayors): family: 20 gp  + 1000 sp  + 1000 cp || personal: 5 gp   + 250 sp  + 250 cp
	Very High class (jarls, lords):      family: 100 gp + 5000 sp  + 5000 cp || personal: 25 gp  + 1250 sp + 1250 cp
	Most High class (kings and royals):  family: 500 gp + 10000 sp + 5000 cp || personal: 125 gp + 2500 cp + 1250 cp

Skilled Hireling:    5 sp/day
Unskilled Hireling:  2 sp/day

Any carved piece is counted outside of the multiplier, and price goes up by 5x.
Example: walls multiplier x5, but 1 is carved. the walls cost 1gp each.
Example: walls cost: (1gp x 5) + carved piece: (1gp x 5)
