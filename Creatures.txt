Daeva Warrior (large fiend):
	Neutral Evil

	Armor Class: 14 (lamellar armor)
	Hit Points:  45 (6d8 + 18)
	Speed:       30ft

	Strength: 		14  +2
	Dexerity: 		12  +1
	Constitution: 	14  +2
	Intelligence: 	10
	Wisdom:			10
	Charisma:		13  +1

	Skills: Animal Handling +3, Deception +2, Persuation +2, Survival +4
	Senses: Darkvision 40m (120ft), Passive Perception 12
	Languages: Abyssal
	Challenge: 3

	Special Traits:
		Night Dweller: When it is day, the daeva deal 2 less damage, and take 2 more damage, and it's Passive Perception becomes 8.

	Abilities:
		Scimitar: Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 2m (6ft), one target. Hit: 9 (1d6 + 5) slashing damage.
		Eat: The daeva consumes a corpse of a human or elf it is standing on. The daeva regains 2d6 hit points.

		Multiattack: The daeva makes two attacks with it's scimitar.

Draugr (medium undead):
	When the soul of a dead person is unable to cross the Astral Plane to the afterlife, it returns back into it's dead body and will wake up as a Draugr 50 to 400 years later.

	Any chaotic alignment

	Armor Class: 14 (chainmail hauberk)
	Hit Points:  38 (4d6 + 24)
	Speed:       30ft

	Strength: 		16  +3
	Dexerity: 		12  +1
	Constitution: 	18  +4
	Intelligence: 	10
	Wisdom:			13  +1
	Charisma:		17  +3

	Skills: Athletics +10, Intimidation +4
	Senses: Darkvision 10m (30ft)
	Languages: The languages it knew in life
	Challenge: 6

	Special Traits:
		Immortal: When the draugr dies, it is reduced to a pile of ashes. After 1d4 minutes, the draugr materializes at the spot which it died, and the ashes disappear. The only way to truly kill the draugr is to kill the draugr while it is under the effects of a Bless spell.

		Undead Nature: The draugr does not require food, water, or rest.

	Abilities:
		Longaxe: Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 2m (6ft), one target. Hit: 14 (1d10 + 8) slashing damage.
		Arming Sword: Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 2m (6ft), one target. Hit: 12 (1d6 + 8) slashing damage.

Gvarth (huge plant):
	Gvarths are sentient trees that looks like a regular tree, created from normal trees by evil fey who believe humanoid races have "ruined" nature, and are basically crusaders to retake the material plane. It travels by having it's roots move in the dirt like milipede legs. Some gvarths may be adorned with plain dark green banners, or carved with sylvan and druidic runes.

	Chaotic Evil

	Armor Class: 16 (natural armor)
	Hit Points:  138 (12d12 + 60)
	Speed:       30ft

	Strength: 		22  +6
	Dexerity: 		10
	Constitution: 	25  +7
	Intelligence: 	4   -3
	Wisdom:			8   -1
	Charisma:		5   -5

	Damage Vulnerabilities: Fire
	Damage Resistances: Bludgeoning, Piercing
	Condition Immunities: Blinded, Grappled, Poisoned, Stunned, Restrained
	Senses: Blindsight 20m (60ft), Truesight 100m (300ft)
	Languages: Can understand Druidic Sylvan, but cannot speak
	Challenge: 7

	Special Traits:
		Deaf: The gvarth is unable to hear.
		Earth Restrictions: The gvarth can only move on dirt, sand and mud.

	Abilities:
		Root Lash: Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5m (15ft), one target. Hit: 10 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage.
		Leap: The gvarth picks a spot with 20m (60ft), 10m (30ft) high. The spot cannot contain a creature Large or bigger. The gvarth then leaps out of the ground, and lands there. All creatures where the gvarth lands take 4d10 Bludgeoning damage. If the creature survive, then it is incapacitated until the gvarth moves.

		Multiattack: The gvarth makes two attacks with Root Lash.
