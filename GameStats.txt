Max Health:      4 + level + constitution modifier 
Damage (spells): Divide by 5 rounded down

Convert dnd stats to realistic roleplay stats (input in feet, results are already converted to meters):
	Walk speed: (spd / 8)
	Run speed:  (spd / 6) * 1.5
