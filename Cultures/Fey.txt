Behaviour patterns:
	Sprite villages are usually small wooden houses made of twigs and thatch built on trees. A tree is usually enough to sustain a sprite village. Sprite villages are never built on trees that have a dryad bound to it.

Preferred weapons:
	Magic

Preferred metals:
	Silver

Gems, gemstones and crystals:
	Crystal/White opal - Associated with Lûn, and believed to be associated with Lûnanyr and life
	Emerald            - Associated with nature
	Jade/Malachite     - Believed to be associated with druidry
	Labadorite         - Associated with Vel, and believed to be associated with Parhrûgi and magic
